About Me

A mind shaped by
ambition & roots

Born on the 28th of March, 1999, in a family with deep commercial heritage spanning decades in Old Dhaka's trading heart, G. K. Anim grew up understanding the value of enterprise and perseverance from an early age.

After excelling through Bangladesh's premier educational institutions and earning a BSc from BRAC University in 2024, he pivoted from corporate IT into the world of marketing — bringing technological precision to creative strategy.

Now channelling years of professional experience into founding a marketing agency, he is carving his identity as an entrepreneur — one campaign at a time.
